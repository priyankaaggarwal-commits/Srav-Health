Use this checklist as a living document

This checklist is not meant to be completed in one sitting. It is a reference you return to as your practice evolves a way of auditing where you are, confirming what is in place, and identifying the one or two items most worth focusing on next. The practitioners who grow consistently are not the ones who do everything at once. They are the ones who do the right things in the right order, without skipping stages.

If you are in Stage 1, resist the pull of Stage 3 tactics. A sophisticated retargeting setup is not what moves the needle when you have 6 clients. A clear positioning statement and a lead magnet does. If you are in Stage 2 and your growth has plateaued, the answer is almost always in the strategy and financial sections not in adding a new channel.
