Practitioner Standards

Who we work with.
Quality is non-negotiable.

We are selective by design. We only partner with practitioners who meet our qualification standards — because our reputation is built on your outcomes.

Education

Formal Ayurvedic Training

  • Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from an accredited institution
  • OR equivalent 3+ year accredited Ayurvedic program in the US (NAMA-recognized schools preferred)
  • Continuing education in women's health, gynecology, or hormonal balance
  • Active membership in NAMA (National Ayurvedic Medical Association) preferred
Clinical Experience

Established Practice

  • Minimum 3 years of clinical practice post-graduation
  • Active client base of at least 20 current or recent clients
  • Women's health as a primary or significant focus area
  • Experience with conditions including hormonal imbalance, perimenopause, stress, gut health, or fertility support
Proof of Outcomes

Demonstrable Results

  • Minimum 5 documented client success stories or case studies
  • At least 3 verifiable client testimonials or reviews
  • Willingness to share outcomes data (anonymized) to support content
  • No active disciplinary actions or unresolved complaints
Geography & Readiness

US-Based, Growth-Ready

  • Currently serving clients in the US, with focus on SF Bay Area or Austin, TX
  • Capacity to take on 3–8 new clients per month
  • Willing to invest 2–3 hours/week in the positioning and content process
  • Open to refining how you communicate your methodology for Western audiences
Our Approach for Ayurveda

How we position
Ayurvedic practitioners.

The positioning challenge is specific

Ayurvedic practitioners face a unique communication challenge in the Western market. Your methodology is 5,000 years deep — but your potential clients are health-conscious, science-literate women in SF and Austin who are skeptical of anything that feels too esoteric, yet hungry for solutions that conventional medicine hasn't provided.

The practitioners who win this market are the ones who can hold both: ancient wisdom and modern evidence. Not watering down the tradition — translating it into language that builds trust before the first consultation.

We specialize in this translation. We help you claim a clear, confident positioning that respects your training and converts your ideal client.

Dosha-Informed Messaging

We build your content around the specific conditions your clients are experiencing — fatigue, hormonal imbalance, perimenopause, gut health — bridging Ayurvedic concepts to felt symptoms without over-simplifying your methodology.

Evidence Bridge

We integrate current research on Ayurvedic interventions alongside your clinical experience. Your ideal client trusts both ancient wisdom and modern evidence. We help you speak both languages fluently.

Community Distribution

SF and Austin have specific communities of health-conscious women. We identify where they congregate online and offline, and build your presence there systematically — not with ads, but with genuine authority content.
