Who It's For

of established practitioners.

Srav Health is not for everyone. We work exclusively with mid-tier integrative women's health practitioners who are ready to scale — and who meet our quality standards.

You're a fit if you —

  • Practice Ayurveda, Functional Medicine, or TCM with a women's health focus
  • Have an established practice with existing clients and real testimonials
  • Are based in or serving clients in the US, particularly SF or Austin
  • Are frustrated with inconsistent client flow despite strong clinical outcomes
  • Are ready to invest in systematic growth — not looking for quick fixes
  • Can commit to a 90-day engagement

We're not the right fit if you —

  • Are just starting out and don't yet have client proof points
  • Want to stay general and serve all health conditions

  • Are not willing to invest time in the positioning process
  • Expect results without collaboration
